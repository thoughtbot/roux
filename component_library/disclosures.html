When are taxes due?

Federal income taxes are typically due on April 15th of each year, unless that date falls on a weekend or holiday, in which case the deadline may be extended to the next business day.

How should I submit my tax return?

You can submit your tax return electronically using e-filing services or by mailing a paper return to the appropriate IRS address.
